Rabi Crops
The crops that are sown in winter season are
called rabi crops. Rabi crops are sown at the
beginning of winter season (October/November)
and are harvested in March-April.Rabi crops do
not depned on monsoon rains for growth.
Wheat, gram, pea, musturd, and linseed are rabi
crops.

Kahrif Crops
The crops that are sown in rainy season are
called kharif crops.Karif crops are sown at the
beginning of the mosoon season (June/July) and
harvested at the end of the monsoon season
(September/October).Kharif crops depend on
monsoon rain for growth.Rice, maize, soyabean,
groundnut, cotton, etc., are kharif crops.
